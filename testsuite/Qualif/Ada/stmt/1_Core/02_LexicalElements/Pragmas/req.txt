Pragmas (ARM 2.8)

%(reqs-headline)s

Different kinds of pragmas exist, some always active, some possibly ignored
depending on configuration parameters.

* Inactive pragmas shall be ignored;

* Active pragmas shall be recognized and handled as simple statements.
