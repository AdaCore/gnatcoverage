Lexical Elements (ARM chapter 2)

%(req-headline)s

The only chapter 2 item relevant to SC assessments are Pragmas, which might
embed executable constructs. Different kinds of pragmas exist, some always
active, some possibly ignored depending on configuration parameters. We obey a
single requirement in this context:

Active pragmas shall be recognized and handled as simple statements: an active
pragma shall be reported uncovered unless reached by execution/elaboration
flow.
