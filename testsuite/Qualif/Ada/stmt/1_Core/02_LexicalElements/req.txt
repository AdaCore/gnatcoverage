Lexical Elements (ARM chapter 2)

%(req-headline)s

The only chapter 2 item relevant to SC assessments are Pragmas, which might
embed executable constructs. Different kinds of pragmas exist, some always
active, some possibly ignored depending on configuration parameters. We obey 
the following requirements in this context:

* Inactive pragmas shall be ignored;

* Active pragmas shall be recognized and handled as simple statements.
