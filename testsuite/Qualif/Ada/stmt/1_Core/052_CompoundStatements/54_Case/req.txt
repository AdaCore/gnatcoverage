Case Statements (ARM 5.4)

%(req-headline)s

A CASE statement is composed by several CASE alternatives and zero or
one OTHERS alternative. Each alternative contains other statements.

The coverage of a CASE statement shall depend on the evaluation of its
alternatives.  If an alternative is not evaluated, then it shall not be
covered. If an alternative is not chosen, then all statements it contains
shall not be covered.

%(tstrategy-headline)s

We check different syntax forms of CASE statement:

* with and without OTHERS alternative;
* alternatives defined by an expression and by a range;
* alternatives containing one value and multiple values.
