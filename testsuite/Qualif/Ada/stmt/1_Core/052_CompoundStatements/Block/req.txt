Block statements (ch. 5.6)

%(req-headline)s

A block statement contains others statements and (optionally) declarations and
exception handlers as its components. If the execution does not reach a block
statement, all its components shall be reported as uncovered.

%(tstrategy-headline)s

* Check various syntax forms of a block statement (with and without a
  declarative part, with and without exception handlers) in different
  contexts;

* Check nested block statements.
