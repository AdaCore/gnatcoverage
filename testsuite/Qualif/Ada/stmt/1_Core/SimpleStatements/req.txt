%(req-headline)s

Simple statements are elements of chapter 5 which are atomic (they do
not contain other statements).

A simple statement shall be reported as uncovered if it is not executed.

%(tstrategy-headline)s

This tool operation requirement is split into subordinate requirements
according to the sections of Chapter 5 (Statements) of the ARM.

* Assign statement    (ch. 5.1)
* Null statement      (ch. 5.2)
* Exit statement      (ch. 5.7)
* Goto statement      (ch. 5.8)
