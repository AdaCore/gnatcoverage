Check that a declaration that contains a discrete subtype definition is
reported as uncovered if the declaration is not elaborated, and it does not
reported as uncovered if the declaration is elaborated. 

Tests use discrete subtype definition in such a way that this discrete
subtype definition shall be the only reason to generate coverage information
to avoid masking of subtype indication coverage with coverage information
generating for another construct.

Different test drivers
choose different declarations of interest to elaborate.
